About me. Sahil Konjarla

First and foremost I am a student. I'm currently a Junior at University of California, Santa Cruz majoring in Computer Science and a minor in Technology and Information Management. Photography is just my hobby, I love to take pictures of whatever catches my eye. I like to dabble in all sorts of styles; nature, studio, and portraits. This website is going to be a display of some of my proudest shots that I've taken in the 6 years that I have been a photographer. It will show the two different methods I use to take pictures a digital camera (Pentax K-3 II), and a film camera (Pentax K1000).
